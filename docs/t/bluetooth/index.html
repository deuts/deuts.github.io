Why Are Bluetooth Speakers Losing Their Built-In Microphones?

Bluetooth speaker makers are removing built-in microphones, frustrating users who rely on them for online meetings. Possible reasons include cost-cutting, market positioning, and underutilization, with significant impacts on mobile users.
